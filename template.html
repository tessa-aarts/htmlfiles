About Bierpaal

Bierpaal is a website created by students, for students. We, as students living in a studenthouse, all understand the extreme amount of trouble it can be to keep track of which of your roommates drank how many beers from which crate. Supposing your roommates are all honest enough to keep track of this, we present to you an easy and fun method. Cheers!

About us

We are four TU/e students with different backgrounds (ICT, Industrial Design and Psychology and Technology), that have in common that we follow the course 'Web Technology', for which we created this site. We all live in a studenthouse in Eindhoven and all enjoy having a beer every now and then.

About beer

As you are visiting this site at this moment, we'll just assume that you also have a passion (or just like) the liquid gold - beer. We hope you enjoy this site, and may your beer be plentiful. Geniet, maar drink met mate.
